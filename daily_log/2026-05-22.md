**fix(semantic): reject unresolved method calls and enforce arity at analysis time (075d3dc).** CONFIRMED. Substantial rewrite of the method-call analysis path in `src/frontend/semantic.rs`. Previously, unresolved method receivers would fall through to the IR layer's mangled-callee miss, producing unhelpful `UnresolvedSemanticArtifact` errors. Now: receivers not in scope get a clear scope error, non-struct receivers are rejected with type information, undefined methods produce a `method '{struct}.{method}' is not defined` diagnostic, and arity is enforced at analysis time mirroring the free-function precedent. Also removed the dead `classify_type` function.
